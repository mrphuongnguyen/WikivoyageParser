Bricktown Center at Charleston (sometimes referred to as the Bricktown Mall) is an open-air lifestyle center located in the Charleston neighborhood of Staten Island, New York City, United States. The center is located at the intersection of Tyrellan Avenue and Veterans Road West.

Bricktown Center at Charleston opened in 2007, encompassing 42 acres of formerly vacant land.[1]

Features[edit]

The anchor tenants in the center are Home Depot, Target, Bed Bath and Beyond, and Christmas Tree Shoppes. The only other tenant is Applebees.[2] Other non-commercial amenities that the developer agreed with the city to provide include a 16-foot wide greenway, which includes bicycle and pedestrian paths, as well as the upgrading of existing infrastructure.[3]

Transportation[edit]

The Bricktown Centre at Charleston is served by the S74 and S78 New York City Transit buses.
